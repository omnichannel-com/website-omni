Questions people ask

Frequently asked questions

What is a Maturity Review?

A Maturity Review is a structured assessment of where your customer experience and service operations sit today, from siloed channels through omnichannel to channelless, and where AI is safe to introduce. You get a clear, prioritised, costed roadmap to improve service, lower cost to serve, and adopt AI with people in control.

Do you only advise, or do you deliver the change too?

Both. We design the change and help deliver it, working alongside your team. We are practitioners who have run contact centres and back offices, not theorists, and we stay independent of any platform vendor.

Which platforms and systems do you work with?

The major contact centre, CRM, and automation platforms, including Genesys, Salesforce, and Zendesk, alongside your core banking, policy, and billing systems. We recommend what fits your operation, not what we resell.

What is human-in-control AI?

Human-in-control AI is a setup where the AI prepares the work but never acts on its own. It drafts the reply, prepares the claim, or runs the check, then a person with the authority to own the outcome approves before anything happens. The AI proposes, the person decides.

How do you keep AI safe in a regulated industry?

A person with authority approves every consequential decision, sensitive cases such as vulnerable customers route to a human by policy, and every step is recorded. You can show the FCA, Ofcom, Ofgem, or an internal auditor exactly what happened and why.

How do engagements start, and how are they priced?

Most engagements begin with a Maturity Review, priced on scope. Delivery work that follows is priced on scope and outcomes. We never recommend something you do not need.
