# OmniChannel

> OmniChannel is human-in-control channel intelligence: AI agents that grow audience, find the right partners, and turn content into compounding distribution, while a human retains authority over every consequential decision. Human in control (HIC) is an operating model for agentic AI in which autonomous agents plan and execute work, while a human retains defined authority to set objectives, approve consequential actions, and intervene or revoke autonomy at any point.

- [What Is Human in Control?](https://omni-channel.com/human-in-control): Canonical definition of human in control (HIC), its three pillars (authority, visibility, revocability), and how it differs from human-in-the-loop and human-on-the-loop.
- [Human in Control vs. Human-in-the-Loop vs. Human-on-the-Loop](https://omni-channel.com/human-in-control-vs-human-in-the-loop): Plain-English comparison of the three oversight models for AI agents, with a worked example.
