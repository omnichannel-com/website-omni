One stroke. One family.

16 · Surfaces · Light + dark

Two surfaces.
One contrast budget.

Every text + surface pairing in the brand clears WCAG 2.2 AA (4.5:1 body, 3:1 large). Mauve and bright-blue accents are reserved for surfaces where the math works. The audit below shows the ratio for each token; reds are AAA, ambers are AA-only.
