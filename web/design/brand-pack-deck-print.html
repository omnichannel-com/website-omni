07 · Buttons

Icons always lead.
Sentence case. One verb.

Open inbox
PRIMARY GRADIENT (1× per page) GHOST TEXT LINK

Sentence case. One verb, one object: "Resolve conversation," not "Resolve your conversations now!". No exclamation, no emoji, no em-dash. Icons always lead at 16px (14px small) with 1.25 stroke.
